Transportation

Kolhapur is well connected to Metro cities like Pune and Mumbai by road. It also connected to Tech Cities like Hyderabad and Mumbai by Air.Even the railways from various part of country arrive at Kolhapur Junction.

Road Structure

Internally it has very good Road infrastructure that connects with every small village of Kolhapur.Every town in the city is connected through good quality roads with proper maintenance.

Industries

Kolhapur is wealthy as well because it holds Three MIDC's(Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation) Area which located at Gokul-Shirgaon,Shiroli and Kagal(5 star).In this MIDC Industries like Raymond,Wilo(pump),Gokul Milk Dairy etc. are Present.
